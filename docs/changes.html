Changes to Wonky Pickins!

Every year we look back at what worked and what could be better! Here's a list of what has changed over the years.

2024/2025 season

  • Defense: Blocked shots increased from 0.25 to 0.5 (points per shot)
  • Enforcers: Hits increased from 0.25 to 0.5 (points per hit)
  • Enforcers: Penalty minutes increased from 0.5 to 1 (points per minute)
