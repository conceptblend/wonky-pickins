Wonky Pickins!

We played around with the options in the hockey pool website and we can actually make things quite wonky with the points system (in addition to the daily wonky pickins question).

Here's what we're thinking: Choose 8 players for your roster, and one team.

Step 1 — Choose 8 players.

Here's how they earn points. Choose wonkily.

  • Forward and Defense points:

    • Goal: 1
    • Assist: 1
    • Power play goal: 0.25 (bonus)
    • Power play assist: 0.25 (bonus)
    • Short-handed goal: 1 (bonus)
    • Short-handed assist: 0.5 (bonus)
    • Game-winning goal: 0.5 (bonus)
    • Hat trick: 1 (bonus)

  • Defense will also get points for:

    • Blocked shots: 0.25 (points per shot)

  • Goalie points:

    • Win: 0 (that's right, no points for a win unless it's a shutout!)
    • Shutout: 1
    • Lose: 1
    • Save: 0.05 (points per save) - e.g. 1 point for every 20 saves

  • Enforcers — Limit 2

    Enforcers only get points for:

    • Hits: 0.25 (points per hit)
    • Penalty minutes: 0.5 (points per minute)

    Also...

    • Any player can be designated as an enforcer.
    • You can choose up to 2 enforcers. This is optional - you don't have to choose any if you don't want to.

Step 2 — Choose 1 team.

It can be any of the teams remaining (it doesn't matter if you picked players on this team or not).

Teams get points for:

  • Win (in regulation or overtime): 1
  • Shutout win: 1 (bonus)

Rules

For each game we'll also do a Wonky Pickins question worth bonus points.

  • We'll send out the question on the group chat each game day morning.
  • Respond with your answer in the chat before the game starts.
  • If you win Wonky Pickins points, they will show up in your total on the pool website the next morning.

If a points tie-breaker is needed, it will be determined by:

  1. Total goals by players in your roster
  2. Power play goals
  3. Game-winning goals
