Step 2 — Choose 1 team.

It can be any of the teams remaining (it doesn't matter if you picked players on this team or not).

Teams get points for:

  • Win (in regulation or overtime): 1
  • Shutout win: 1 (bonus)

Rules

For each game we'll also do a Wonky Pickins question worth bonus points.

  • We'll send out the question on the group chat each game day morning.
  • Respond with your answer in the chat before the game starts.
  • If you win Wonky Pickins points, they will show up in your total on the pool website the next morning.

If a points tie-breaker is needed, it will be determined by:

  1. Total goals by players in your roster
  2. Power play goals
  3. Game-winning goals
